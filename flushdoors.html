Flush Doors

Smooth surface for your custom touches

Types

APEX Flush Doors are available in two types:
Commercial: With face veneers from selected commercial hardwoods.
Decorative: With one or both sideface veneers from decorative timbers like Teek

Phenol Bonding

The blockboard core. cross brands and face veneers are bonded together under heat and pressure using a BWP type Phenol Formaldehyde synthetic resin adhesive. APEX Flush Doors generally conform to IS:2202 (Part 1):1991, types BN and DN.

Outstanding Features

High strength. stiffness and impact resistance. Durability against high moisture conditions and cyclic changes in weather. Dimensional stability, high degree of consistency in quality, assured standardize industrial procesing techniques and quality control systems are what make APEX Flush Doors outstanding.
